Our Technology

Advanced Plasma Physics

Our proprietary plasma technology enables precise control of plasma parameters for optimal processing conditions. Dudo Proton's plasma systems deliver:

  • Ultra-high density plasma generation for faster processing
  • Precise energy control for minimal substrate damage
  • Advanced pulsed plasma techniques for challenging materials
  • Multi-zone plasma control for uniform processing across large wafers

We've developed unique solutions for challenging plasma processes, including high aspect ratio etching and ultra-thin film deposition critical for next-generation semiconductor devices.
